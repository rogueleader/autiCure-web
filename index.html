WHAT IS AUTISM?
The definition of Autism and Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is provided by the Diagnostic and Statistical manual of Mental Disorders (DSM-IV). Children with autism are less able to interact with the world as other children do. Typically they have deficits in three key areas: Verbal and non-verbal Communication Social awareness and interactions Imaginative play (variable interests and behaviours).

AutiCure is a web-app designed to identify autism in childrens at the comfort of their home . Our aim is to eradicate the ramifications of this disorder by identifying it at early stages

Autism detection at early age .Many times parents hesitate to visit a doctor and are confused about their child. This software would be help them to detect disease at their home.
